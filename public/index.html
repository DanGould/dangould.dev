This is a responsive WordPress website built to fit the developing needs of my local fire department. The chief can now post images and stories as well as other event updates on his own without the need for a trained webmaster. The custom theme fits the department's aesthetic. The site enables integration of published posts with the departments popular Facebook page. It was built exclusively on volunteer hours as my Eagle Scout Service project.
